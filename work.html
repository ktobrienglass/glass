Intro

I use stained glass to figure out how visual stimuli, such as colour, light and scale, can trigger the emotion of awe. I'm interested in perception and how the mind shapes what we see. I come from a Fine Art background and now study psychology and neuroscience. But I also just like cats. For more info, visit my About page.
